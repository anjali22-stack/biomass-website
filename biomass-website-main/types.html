Types of Biomass

Biomass can be converted into energy through a variety of sources:

  • Wood & Forest Residue – Common fuel for heating and electricity.
  • Agricultural Waste – Crop leftovers, sugarcane bagasse, coconut shells.
  • Animal Manure – Used for biogas production.
  • Organic Municipal Waste – Food scraps and biodegradable trash.
  • Algae Biomass – A highly efficient new source of biofuel.
